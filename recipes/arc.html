Arroz Caldo Recipe


Description

Arroz Caldo is a tasty Filipino rice porridge dish that has many names, usuallly served when someone is sick, can be eaten as a snack also.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tblsp olive oil
  • 1 diced onion
  • 2 crushed garlic cloves
  • 1 2 inch fresh ginger, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 2 1/4 pounds chicken wings, split, and tips discarded
  • 1 tblsp fish sauce
  • 5 1/4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup glutinous sweet rice
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 green onion, chopped
  • 1 lemon, sliced (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon fish sauce (optional)

Steps:

  1. Heat oil in large pot over medium heat
  2. Cook and stir onion, garlic, and ginger in hot oil until fragrant (5 minutes)
  3. Add chicken wings, cook and stir 1 mn
  4. Stir in fish sauce, cover, and cook for 2 mns
  5. Pour Chicken broth into pot, then stir in sweet rice and bring mixture to boil
  6. Cover and cook for 10mns stirring occasionally to prevent rice from sticking to bottom of the pot
  7. Season with salt and pepper, portion soup into bowls and garnish with green onion
  8. Serve with lemon slices and fish sauce if desired
