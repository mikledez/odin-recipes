Adobo Chicken Recipe


Description

Chicken Adobo is a traditional Filipino dish of braised chicken in a sweet and tangy sauce, made with vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, and bay leaves. Usually eaten with rice.

Ingredients:

  • Skinless chicken thighs
  • 1/3 cup soy sauce (not dark)
  • 1/3 cup + 2 tbsp white vinegar
  • 3 minced garlic cloves
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 small diced brown onion
  • 1 tbsp whole black pepper
  • 2 tbsp sugar (preferably brown)
  • 4 bay leaves
  • 2 green onion sliced
  • 1 1/2 cup water

Steps:

  1. Combine chicken soy sauce, minced garlic, vinegar, and bay leaves in bowl.. Marinate for at least 20 minutes.
  2. Heat 1 tbsp oil in skillet high heat.
  3. Remove chicken from marinade and place in pan, sear both sides until brown (do not cook chicken all the way through)
  4. Remove chicken from skillet and set aside
  5. Heat remaining oil in skillet, then add garlic and onion, cook for 1 1/2 minutes
  6. Add reserved marinade, water, sugar, and black pepper
  7. Bring it to a simmer, then turn heat to medium high. Simmer 5 minutes.
  8. Add chicken smooth side down. Simmer uncovered 20-25 mns. Flip chicken at around 15mns until sauce reduces to jam-like syrup.
  9. If sauce isn't thick enough then remove chicken and let sauc simmer by itself
  10. Coat chicken in glaze then serve over rice
